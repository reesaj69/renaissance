Welcome to RECS

A Nonprofit Organization formed to help students increase their academic achievement in the areas of STEAM and life skills to allow them to maximize their potential in becoming productive citizens of our global society.

Meet Our Board Members


Teresa Jackson, CEO

Teresa is the CEO of Renaissance. She is a Math teacher, certified in the state of Georgia, with more than 15 years of experience in all middle and high school level mathematics courses. Her motto is "All students can learn if they are in an environment that is conducive to learning"!

Stephanie Ivery, CFO

Stephanie is the CFO of Renaissance and the senior builder of Ivery Steps Enrichment and Tutoring. She has more than 15 years of tutoring and teaching experience. She has a chemical engineering background. Her motto that she lives and instructs by is "Simply Building Learners!"

Jermel Byrd, Secretary

Jermel is our secretary who handles the daily operations of Renaissance. She is an Engineering/Computer Science educator with more than 15 years experience. Her motto is "Education is the fiber of your every being, use that education to make the world a better place"!
